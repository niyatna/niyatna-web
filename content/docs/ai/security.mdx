Agent safety in Niyatna is operational, not decorative.

The goal is to let useful work move while keeping irreversible, sensitive, or external actions under human control.

## Gate classes

- **Allowed**: local reversible edits, read-only inspection, builds, tests, screenshots.
- **Approval required**: publish, deploy, external send, billing, account/security settings, destructive database changes.
- **Blocked**: actions outside the stated intent or without enough context.

## Proof requirements

Sensitive work should return a clear trail: what was attempted, what succeeded, what failed, and what still needs a human decision.
