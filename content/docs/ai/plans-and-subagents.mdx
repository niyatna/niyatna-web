Plans turn a large intent into smaller proof-bearing steps.

Subagents are useful when the work has independent streams: research, code, visual QA, copy, or source comparison. Each stream must return evidence, not just a summary.

## Plan shape

1. What will be changed?
2. What will be preserved?
3. What evidence proves the step?
4. What needs approval?

## Subagent output

A subagent result should include paths, commands, URLs, screenshots, or source references that can be checked.
