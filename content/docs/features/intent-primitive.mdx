The intent primitive (captured in the intent console) is where a vague request becomes a real work contract.

It captures what the person wants, what constraints matter, what can be changed, what needs approval, and what proof must come back.

<img src="/terminal.webp" alt="Niyatna intent console" width="2560" height="1600" />

## What it captures

- Goal and desired outcome
- Context and relevant files or sources
- Constraints, taste, risk, and boundaries
- Acceptance criteria
- Permission level
- Proof standard

## Why it matters

Agents are powerful but literal. If the intent is weak, the work drifts. The primitive makes the initial contract explicit before execution begins.
