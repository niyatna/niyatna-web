Human agency (the workspace theme) keeps the original visual DNA intact: dark glass surfaces, animated line waves, soft motion, and focused hierarchy.

The visual style is designed to keep Niyatna calm, focused, and inspectable.

<img src="/themes.webp" alt="Niyatna visual system" width="2560" height="1600" />

## Design rules

- Preserve the line-wave atmosphere.
- Use motion to clarify state, not distract.
- Keep the human decision context visible.
- Avoid generic AI-purple landing-page patterns.
- Make proof and action hierarchy obvious.

## Why this matters

Trust is visual too. A chaotic interface makes it harder to understand what the agent did. Niyatna should feel calm enough to review serious work.
