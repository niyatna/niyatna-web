Niyatna is the framework for building the **Agentic Company**.

Every real company starts with a problem worth solving. From that problem comes **intent**. With intent you get a **dream**. From the dream, a **mission**. From the mission, a **vision**. From the vision, **goals**. And only then—goals get delegated to **specialized AI agents**.

## The Intent Chain

| Step | What It Is | Why It Matters |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Problem** | A real issue civilization needs solved | Without a problem, there is no company |
| **Intent** | The decision to solve it | The human anchor for everything |
| **Dream** | The world after the problem is solved | Defines the direction |
| **Mission** | What you commit to doing | Creates accountability |
| **Vision** | How it looks when it works | Makes the abstract concrete |
| **Goals** | Measurable, delegatable targets | What agents actually execute |
| **Agents** | Specialized autonomous execution | The leverage layer |

---

## What Is an Agentic Company?

It is not a Kanban board with a chatbot. It is not "AI-powered productivity." An Agentic Company is a modern standard where every department—Finance, Marketing, Sales, Operations, Engineering—can be run by specialized AI agents.

Each agent has its own:
- **Personality** — How it communicates and decides
- **Mission & Goals** — What it exists to accomplish
- **Boundaries** — What it is and is not allowed to do
- **Responsibilities & Targets** — What it is accountable for
- **Capabilities & Specialization** — What it is good at
- **Knowledge Base** — What it knows
- **Tools, MCP Skills, Slash Commands** — What it can use

But none of that matters if the company itself has no intent. The agents are not the company. The intent is the company. The agents are the leverage.

---

## Why This Matters Now

Traditional companies scale by hiring more people. That works, but it is slow, expensive, and creates operational drag. The Agentic Company scales by delegating goals to autonomous agents that execute within strict permission boundaries—while the human retains full strategic authority.

This is not just for enterprises with existing teams. It is for anyone with a real problem worth solving and the intent to build a solution around it.

---

## How It Works

1. **Start with the problem** — Define the real issue your company exists to solve.
2. **Map the intent chain** — From problem through dream, mission, vision, to concrete goals.
3. **Design the agents** — Build specialized agents for each goal with clear boundaries and verification requirements.
4. **Activate verification** — Every agent action returns with proof before it asks for trust.
5. **Operate and evolve** — Monitor, optimize, and recalibrate as your company grows.
