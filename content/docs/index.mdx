Niyatna is the **agentic-company formation system**: the integrated operating stack, standards, rules, gates, and interfaces for forming and running agentic companies.

Every real company starts with a problem worth solving. From that problem comes human **intent**. With intent you get a **dream**. From the dream, a **mission**. From the mission, a **vision**. From the vision, **goals**. And only then—goals get delegated to **specialized AI agents**.

> The agents are not the company. The intent is the company. The agents are the leverage.

---

## Core Brand Architecture

The Niyatna ecosystem is structured into five integrated layers:

*   **OpenIntent:** The protocol and specification layer defining structured intent packets, interoperable goal structures, and message routing.
*   **Proof of Intent:** The verification primitive and cryptographic validation engine that proves delegated execution maps back to human intent.
*   **Niyatna OS:** The runtime operating layer connecting agents, durable memory, sandboxed tools, dashboards, channels, and permission rules.
*   **Niyatna Agents:** The specialized workforce execution layer. Agents operate with unique identities, goals, credentials, wallets, and boundaries.
*   **Niyatna HQ:** The control surface and visual dashboard for human operators to inspect execution trails, review proof, and exercise veto power.

## The Intent Chain

In an Agentic Company, human intent is translated into autonomous execution through a rigorous chain:

| Layer | Component | Function |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Problem** | Market Drag | A real friction or challenge that civilization needs solved. |
| **Intent** | Human Decision | The explicit human command and taste bounds guiding the solution. |
| **Dream** | Visionary Target | The post-problem state of the world that the company works toward. |
| **Mission** | Core Commitment | The strategic accountability contract established by the founders. |
| **Vision** | Operational Map | The concrete architecture of how the company functions when successful. |
| **Goals** | Delegated Targets | Measurable, scoped targets assigned to the execution layer. |
| **Agents** | Specialized Workforce | Autonomous execution units that process goals within secure boundaries. |

## Why This Standard Matters

When anyone can solve problems with AI, the scarce thing becomes the operating system to organize it. 

Traditional scaling introduces massive operational drag: headcount growth, communication overhead, shared credentials, and coordination blockages. Niyatna OS solves this by scaling execution through secure, sandboxed agent workforces while anchoring all operations back to verified human intent.
