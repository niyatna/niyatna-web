Niyatna is the **agentic-company formation system**: the integrated operating stack, standards, rules, gates, and interfaces for forming and running agentic companies.

Under Niyatna OS, your business commands are compiled into structured **intent** packets, executed by **specialized agents** inside secure sandboxes, and returned as verifiable **proof** to operate a governed **company**.

> The agents are not the company. The intent is the company. The agents are the leverage.

---

## Core Brand Architecture

The Niyatna ecosystem is structured into five integrated layers:

*   **OpenIntent:** The protocol standard defining structured intent packets, freezing budget ceilings and security constraints before runtimes begin.
*   **Proof of Intent:** The verification engine producing cryptographic receipts (git diffs, build logs, and database checks) to prove execution matches your intent.
*   **Niyatna OS:** The runtime operating layer connecting sandboxed runtimes, vector memory networks, credential vaults, and team communication channels.
*   **Niyatna Agents:** The specialized B2B workforce layer. Agents run autonomously inside sandboxes with dedicated profiles and scoped access.
*   **Niyatna HQ:** The command center and dashboard for human operators to monitor active pipelines, review logs, and exercise absolute veto power.

In an Agentic Company, strategic intent scales execution through a secure, structured loop:

| Layer | Component | Business Function |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Intent** | OpenIntent | The locked target constraints and budgets guiding the execution. |
| **Agents** | Specialized Workforce | Jailed digital workers executing B2B tasks inside isolated sandboxes. |
| **Proof** | Proof of Intent | Inspectable diffs, builds, and logs returned for veto approval. |

## Why This Standard Matters

When anyone can build AI tools, the scarce thing is not code. It is the operating system to organize them safely.

Traditional expansion relies on payroll scaling, which multiplies management drag, office overhead, and credential risks. Niyatna OS solves this by scaling your operations through hardware-isolated agent workforces, allowing you to grow throughput while retaining 100% data privacy and strategic command.
